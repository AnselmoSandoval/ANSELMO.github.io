Electrical Installation and Maintenance (EIM) Strand

EIM Strand

The EIM (Electrical Installation and Maintenance) strand is part of the Technical-Vocational track, and it is an industrial art. The students are specialized with analysing signs, electrical symbols and data, interpretation of technical drawings and plans, maintaining of electric hydraulic tools, installation of electric metallic tubing, wire ways and cabletrays; installing telephone terminal cabinet, cable Bridge. And also the selection of wiring devices used for floor and ground fault current interrupter, which is based from the Philippine Electrical Code standards.

Major Subject: Electrical Installation and Maintenance (EIM)

The major subject of the EIM strand, Electrical Installation and Maintenance, focuses on developing students' expertise in the intricate field of electrical systems, equipping them with the technical proficiency to install, troubleshoot, and maintain electrical infrastructure, ensuring efficient and safe operation in various settings.

National Certificate 1/2/3 for EIM Students

EIM students have the opportunity to earn a National Certificate, recognizing their mastery of electrical installation and maintenance skills, validating their expertise in areas such as electrical wiring, circuit troubleshooting, equipment maintenance, and adherence to safety protocols, thus enhancing their career prospects in the electrical industry.

Courses for EIM Students

BS in Industrial Engineering

BS in Industrial Engineering

The Bachelor of Science in Industrial Engineering (IE) program is designed to prepare the student for professional work in the design, improvement, installation, and maintenance of integrated systems of people, materials, information, equipment, methods, and energy.

BS in Electrical Engineering

BS in Electrical Engineering

The Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering is the branch of engineering that deals with the technology of electricity. Electrical engineers work on a wide range of components, devices and systems, from tiny microchips to huge power station generators.

BS in Electronic Engineering

BS in Electronic Engineering

The Bachelor of Science in Electronic Engineering (BSECE) is a four-year program which supports various tracks including communications, microelectronics, and biomedical engineering, with addition of artificial intelligence and scientific computing.
