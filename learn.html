Welcome to AASMNHS Senior High School Department

Rowell Sandoval

Rowell Sandoval, a visionary leader and former mayor of Mabini, made a significant impact as the founder of AASMNHS. Recognizing the pressing need for quality education in the community, he took the initiative to establish the town's very first public high school. The groundbreaking journey began in 1999, thanks to Rowell's unwavering dedication and relentless efforts. His visionary leadership paved the way for countless students to access education, empowering them with the knowledge and skills necessary to build a brighter future. Rowell Sandoval's remarkable legacy as the founder of AASMNHS continues to inspire generations, leaving an indelible mark on the educational landscape of Mabini.

Located at P. Balibaguhan, Mabini, Batangas

Situated in P. Balibaguhan, Mabini, Batangas, the location of AASMNHS offers numerous advantages as it enhances the educational journey of its students by providing convenience, community engagement, and a deeper connection to their surroundings. The proximity of the school ensures convenient access for the students, allowing them to save valuable time and energy on daily commutes. Furthermore, the close proximity of the school enables students to engage more actively in extracurricular activities and participate in community-based programs. They can readily collaborate with local organizations, businesses, and experts who may be willing to provide mentorship, internship opportunities, or resources to enhance their learning experience.
