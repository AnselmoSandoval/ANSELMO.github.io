National Certificate 1/2/3 for H.E Students

The National Certificate for Home Economics students validates their mastery of essential skills in various areas such as food preparation and cooking, textile and clothing technology, household services, and entrepreneurship, empowering them to pursue diverse career paths in culinary arts, hospitality management, fashion and textile industry, or establish their own home-based enterprises with confidence.

Courses for H.E Students

BS in Culinary Arts

BS in Culinary Arts

The Bachelor of Science in Culinary Arts is a four-year degree program that covers everything from the preparation, cooking, plating, presentation and serving of food.

BS in Interior Designing

BS in Interior Designing

The Bachelor of Science in Interior Designing is a program that focuses on creating functional and visually appealing interior spaces. It covers design principles, space planning, color theory, furniture design, and CAD software.

BS in Nutrition

BS in Nutrition

The Bachelor of Science in Nutrition is a program that focuses on the study of food, dietetics, and human nutrition. Graduates can pursue careers as nutritionists, dietitians, or work in various healthcare and food-related industries.

BS in Tourism Management

BS in Tourism Management

The Bachelor of Science in Tourism Management is a program focused on studying the tourism and hospitality industry. It prepares students for careers in hotels, travel agencies, event management, and tourism marketing.

BS in Hospitality and Restaurant Management

BS in Hospitality and Restaurant Management

The Bachelor of Science in Hospitality and Restaurant Management is a four-year degree program that covers the process of planning, development, human resource management of the different aspects of the hotel, restaurant, and resorts operations.
