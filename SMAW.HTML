Information and Communication Technology (ICT) Strand

ICT Strand

The ICT (Information and Communication Technology) strand focuses on computer systems servicing and other related skills. Students in this strand learn about hardware and software troubleshooting, network setup and maintenance, and other fundamental ICT concepts.

Major Subject: Computer System Servicing (CSS)

Computer System Servicing is a major subject in the ICT strand that provides students with the knowledge and skills needed to assemble, disassemble, maintain, and troubleshoot computer systems and peripherals. Students learn about hardware and software installation, configuration, and diagnostics.

National Certificate 1/2/3 for ICT Students

The National Certificate (NC) program offers ICT strand students the opportunity to gain industry-recognized certifications, validating their skills and knowledge in the field of Information and Communications Technology.

BS in Industrial Technology
